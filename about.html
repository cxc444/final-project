About

Our U-Boat Research project was created as part of WWU CSCI 197: Introduction to Digital Humanities taught by Dustin O'Hara in the fall of 2020.The dataset used comes from the New Jersey Maritime Museum. We analysed the data points that could be connected to German U-Boats found within the NJ Maritime Museum data set and sought to understand more about the effects of their presence in the region. We subtracted much of the dataset in our analysis, and so both datasets will be provided below.

  • New Jersey Maritime Museum Shipwreck Data Set
  • CSCI 197 U-Boat Data Set

Additionally, a blog website was made to document our progress throughout the class. This can also be found below.
